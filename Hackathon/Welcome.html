Innovation at its peak

Sanny-Man derived from the Australian colloquialisms of Sanitiser and a pun on Spider-Man. The small, Australian start up of four to invented this solution after a frustration of a hand sanitiser machine not working in the local mall. Not only does it relief a real consumer pain point, we strive to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG). of 6 - Clean water and sanitation. At the unison of technology and sanitation, we are able to collect, untouched data to help Australia as a whole, work towards universal sanitation.
